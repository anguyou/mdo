It's no secret that I love Rdio—I tweet about it all the time! To keep my tunes on at all times and out of my way, I keep Rdio open with an instance of Fluid, a stand-alone (or site-specific) browser.

One of my favorite features of Fluid? Custom dock icons (and user styles!) for each site-specifc browser. I've been using Wilson Miner's great looking icon, but something felt off to me when placed next to my Twitter icon in the dock. So, I created my own!

It's higher contrast, uses their logo's colors for the background gradient, and it matches my (unreleased) Twitter dock icon—yay! Give it a download!
